\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Solves the one-\/dimensional heat equation for a given material and heat source. 

A class to solve and visualize the 1D heat equation for a material.

Represents the physical properties of a material related to heat transfer.

class representing the heat source F(x,t)

This struct models the thermal properties of a material, including its name, thermal conductivity, density, and specific heat capacity. These properties are crucial in solving heat transfer problems.
